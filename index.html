Our Mission

Based on the statement of God Almighty, “O people! We created you all from a male
and a female, and we made you into nations and tribes so that you may know one
another” (Surah Al Hujrat Ayah 13). The Islamic Center (NIC) plays a vital and holistic
role in bringing the views of the different spectrums of Islamic society together by
creating a common language of dialogue without violating the right Islamic rules.
